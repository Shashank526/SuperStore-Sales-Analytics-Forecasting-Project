# SuperStore-Sales-Analytics-Forecasting-Project
This project analyzes SuperStore retail sales data to uncover valuable business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, regional performance, product categories, and future sales trends. Using Power BI, an interactive dashboard was developed to help decision-makers monitor key business metrics.

Presentation Link : https://gamma.app/docs/Untitled-qwoaraf709x1du3?mode=doc

# Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day. However, transforming raw data into actionable insights can be challenging.

This project aims to:

Analyze sales and profit performance
Identify high-performing products and regions
Understand customer purchasing patterns
Track key business KPIs
Forecast future sales trends
Support strategic decision-making

# Key Insights
Customer Segment Analysis
Consumer Segment contributes the highest share of sales.
Corporate and Home Office segments provide additional revenue opportunities.
Regional Analysis
Western Region generates the highest sales.
Eastern Region also contributes significantly to overall revenue.
Product Analysis
Office Supplies is the top-performing category.
Phones, Chairs, and Binders are among the highest-selling sub-categories.
Shipping Analysis
Standard Class is the most preferred shipping mode.
Trend Analysis
Sales and profits generally increased over time.
Seasonal fluctuations can be observed in monthly sales patterns.


# Business Recommendations
Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments.
Expand successful strategies from top-performing regions.
Monitor discount-heavy products to improve profitability.
Use forecasting insights for inventory optimization.
Continue tracking KPIs to identify growth opportunities.
